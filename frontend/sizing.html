How to Find Your Perfect Fit

Measure Your Foot
Place a ruler on a flat surface. Stand on it and measure from your heel to the tip of your longest toe. Record the measurement in centimeters.
Check Existing Shoe
Look at your favorite shoe that fits perfectly. Check the size on the inside. SOLE sizing is true to size, so it should match your usual size.
Thumb Rule
Wear your SOLE with socks. There should be about a thumb's width of space between your longest toe and the shoe's end. No heel slipping.
Break In Period
Most SOLE shoes have a 24-48 hour break-in period. Wear them around the house first to get them molded to your feet.
Return Policy
Not the right fit? We offer 30-day returns on all sizes. Contact us if you need to exchange for a different size.
